About Us

Welcome to the official website of the Bokaro Steel Employees' (Calcutta) Co-operative Credit Society Ltd, a cornerstone in the financial and social welfare of current and former employees of the Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL), particularly those associated with the Bokaro Steel Plant. Established in 1979 under Registration No. 31/Cal, our society has continuously strived to empower its members through financial support, welfare initiatives, and community-building efforts. Over the years, we have evolved to meet the changing needs of our members, always guided by the principles of mutual aid, cooperation, and social responsibility.

In 2023, we took a significant step forward by establishing the BOSEC Welfare Trust under the Trust Act. This trust, with its General Council comprising members and life members of the Bokaro Steel People Welfare Association, embodies our commitment to the long-term welfare of our community. The association, registered under the Societies Act with Registration No. S017807 on 28/10/2023, plays a pivotal role in representing the interests of both current and former employees of SAIL and their families.

Our mission is to foster a sense of belonging and security among our members, ensuring that they have access to financial resources, social support, and opportunities for personal and professional growth. The BOSEC Welfare Trust is designed to extend these benefits to all eligible beneficiaries, including the family members of SAIL employees, making sure that no one is left behind.

We are proud of our heritage and the role we play in the lives of our members. Our commitment to their welfare is unwavering, and we continuously strive to enhance the benefits and services we offer. As we look to the future, we remain dedicated to building a stronger, more resilient community, one that supports its members at every stage of their lives. Thank you for being a part of our journey.

Membership Eligibility

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Present and past employees of SAIL, Bokaro Steel Plant.
  • Residents of the association’s operational area.
  • Individuals of reputable character, sound mind, and without criminal convictions.
  • Agreement to abide by the association’s constitution and by-laws.

Membership Categories:

  • Founder Members: Signatories in the initial application with a contribution of Rs. 10,000.
  • Life Members: Individuals contributing Rs. 40,000 (Rs. 2,000 for Bokaro Steel Employees’ Co-operative Credit Society members) with an annual renewal fee of Rs. 100.
  • General Members: Individuals approved by the Committee with an annual fee of Rs. 240.
  • Associate Members: Dependent family members with an annual fee of Rs. 250.
  • Affiliate Members: Registered agencies with an annual fee of Rs. 1,200.
  • Honorary Members: Individuals nominated by the Committee for their eminent service, exempt from membership fees.
  • Co-Opted Members: Appointed by the Committee for special purposes.

Refund and Cancellation Policy

No Refund and No Cancellation Policy

At Bokaro Steel People Welfare Association (BSPWA), we prioritize the integrity and sustainability of our association. Therefore, we have established a strict policy regarding refunds and cancellations.

Membership Fees:

All membership fees, including those for Founder Members, Life Members, General Members, Associate Members, and Affiliate Members, are non-refundable. Once paid, these fees cannot be refunded under any circumstances.

Donations and Contributions:

Donations and contributions made to the BSPWA are non-refundable. We appreciate your support and understanding that these funds are immediately put to use for the association’s activities and initiatives.

Event Registrations:

Fees paid for event registrations, workshops, seminars, and other BSPWA events are non-refundable. Cancellations or non-attendance will not entitle participants to any form of refund.

Cancellations:

Membership cancellations are not permitted. Once you become a member of BSPWA, your membership is valid for the period specified without the option for cancellation.

By making a payment or donation to BSPWA, you acknowledge and accept this no refund and no cancellation policy. If you have any questions or require further clarification, please contact us.
