Privacy Policy

Introduction

BOSEC (Bokaro Steel People Welfare Association – BSPWA) respects the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the BOSEC Mobile Application, available on the Google Play Store. This application communicates securely with backend services hosted at: https://mobile.bspwa.in/

Information We Collect

Personal Information
  • Name
  • Phone number and email address
  • Member ID or MIN Number
  • Membership, subscription, and insurance details
  • Family details (if provided)
  • Uploaded photographs (optional)
Authentication Information

The app supports PIN-based login and may offer optional device-based biometric authentication. Biometric data is processed by the device operating system and is not collected, stored, or transmitted by the BOSEC application or its servers.

Payment Information

All payments are processed through secure third-party payment gateways. BOSEC does not store card, UPI, or banking credentials.

Usage & Technical Information

Basic technical data such as IP address and app interaction logs may be collected to ensure application security, performance, and reliability.

How We Use Information

Information is used strictly for:

  • Membership and insurance application processing
  • User authentication and account access
  • Subscription and premium payments
  • Transaction history and receipt generation
  • Customer support and service communication

Data Sharing

BOSEC does not sell or rent personal data. Information may be shared only with:

  • Payment gateway providers
  • Authorized service providers assisting in app operations

All third parties are bound by confidentiality obligations.

App Integrity & Security

The app may use Google Play services to verify legitimate installation and improve performance. No data is used for advertising or profiling.

Data Security

  • Encrypted communication (HTTPS / TLS)
  • Restricted access to authorized systems only

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as required for membership, insurance, and legal compliance.

User Rights

Users may request:

  • Access to their personal data
  • Correction of incorrect information
  • Deletion of their account and associated data

Children’s Privacy

This application is intended for users aged 18 years and above. It does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
