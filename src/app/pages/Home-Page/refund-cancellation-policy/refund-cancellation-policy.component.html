Refund and Cancellation Policy

No Refund and No Cancellation Policy

At Bokaro Steel People Welfare Association (BSPWA), we prioritize the integrity and sustainability of our association. Therefore, we have established a strict policy regarding refunds and cancellations.

Membership Fees:

All membership fees, including those for Founder Members, Life Members, General Members, Associate Members, and Affiliate Members, are non-refundable. Once paid, these fees cannot be refunded under any circumstances.

Donations and Contributions:

Donations and contributions made to the BSPWA are non-refundable. We appreciate your support and understanding that these funds are immediately put to use for the association’s activities and initiatives.

Event Registrations:

Fees paid for event registrations, workshops, seminars, and other BSPWA events are non-refundable. Cancellations or non-attendance will not entitle participants to any form of refund.

Cancellations:

Membership cancellations are not permitted. Once you become a member of BSPWA, your membership is valid for the period specified without the option for cancellation.

By making a payment or donation to BSPWA, you acknowledge and accept this no refund and no cancellation policy. If you have any questions or require further clarification, please contact us.
